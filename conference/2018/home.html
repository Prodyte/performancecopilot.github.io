Where? When?

Tokyo Bay Ariake Washington Hotel
Maple Room

Friday 22nd June
9am - 5pm

The inaugral Performance Co-Pilot conference is bringing together developers and performance analysts to collaborate and learn about recent and future PCP developments.

Co-located with the Linux Foundation Open Source Summit in Japan, this conference also has a deeply technical focus.

Your attendence is free of charge thanks to our generous sponsors, and all attendees must be registered .

The list of speakers and attendees is the responsibility of the organising committee . Registration is gated by the space available and collaborative needs of the PCP developers, so please register early.
